# ARIS_PRODUCT_VISION

## Core value

- Save time.
- Reduce errors.
- Replace repetitive operational work.

## Product direction

- Personal/local assistant.
- Operational automation.
- Enterprise middleware.
- Better than copy-paste workflows.
- Security, auditability, and predictability as differentiators.

## What ARIS is not yet

- Not SaaS-ready.
- Not multi-tenant ready.
- Not free-form automation runtime.
- Not a job-replacement promise.
- Not a generic chatbot.
- Not an enterprise product without the gates.

## Long-term opportunities

- Personal automation.
- Enterprise automation.
- Audit-ready operations.
- Document integration.
- Rich UI.
- Model gateway.
- Local/cloud hybrid.
- Compliance and auditability.

## Constraints

- No premature commercial claims.
- Keep safety, privacy, and licenses in view.
- Tenant/RBAC/cloud remain deferred until their own gates.
